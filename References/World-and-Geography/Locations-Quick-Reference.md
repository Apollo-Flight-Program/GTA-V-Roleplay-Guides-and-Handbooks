# Locations Quick Reference

A fast-reference guide to key locations in Los Santos and Blaine County for roleplay purposes. Covers the primary hubs of activity for each faction type and the IC significance of major landmarks.

---

## Los Santos — City Districts

### Downtown / Pillbox Hill
**IC significance:** The civic and financial heart of the city.

| Location | IC Purpose |
|---|---|
| Pillbox Hill Medical Center (LSMC) | Primary hospital. EMS transport destination, medical RP, DOA scenes. |
| Maze Bank Tower | Tallest skyscraper. Corporate RP, high-value targets, dramatic meets. |
| The Pacific Standard Bank | The most prominent banking institution downtown. High-value robbery target. |
| FIB Building | Federal investigation bureau. Serious organized crime investigations. |

### Mission Row
**IC significance:** Law enforcement hub of the city.

| Location | IC Purpose |
|---|---|
| MRPD (Mission Row Police Department) | Primary LSPD station. Bookings, briefings, patrols. |
| Courthouse (Rockford Hills) | All formal legal proceedings. Arraignments, trials, sentencing. |
| LSPD Impound | Vehicle recovery, impound RP. |

### Vinewood / Rockford Hills
**IC significance:** Entertainment, luxury, and political influence.

| Location | IC Purpose |
|---|---|
| Rockford Hills residential | Wealthy characters. Political figures, celebrities, high-value criminal targets. |
| Vinewood Sign area | Tourist and civilian RP. Also criminal activity — kidnappings, high-profile meets. |
| The Kortz Center | Isolated, elevated. Meetings that need privacy and a dramatic view. |

### Vespucci / Del Perro
**IC significance:** Social hub and boardwalk culture.

| Location | IC Purpose |
|---|---|
| Del Perro Pier | Casual social RP, civilian activity, occasional crime. |
| Vespucci Beach | Open social space. Passive civilian RP. |
| LSIA | All aviation RP. Commercial, private, law enforcement, and emergency flight operations. |

### South Los Santos (Davis / Strawberry / Chamberlain Hills)
**IC significance:** Working-class neighborhoods with significant gang presence.

| Location | IC Purpose |
|---|---|
| Davis Avenue area | Street-level gang territory. Patrol hotspot for LSPD. |
| Chamberlain Hills | Dense residential. Gang operations, domestic calls, community RP. |
| The Forum Drive area | Classic territory dispute zone. Patrol, criminal, community RP. |

### East Los Santos (La Mesa / El Burro Heights / Cypress Flats)
**IC significance:** Industrial zone. Criminal logistics and LEO industrial patrol.

| Location | IC Purpose |
|---|---|
| La Mesa industrial | Chop shops, warehouse stashes, criminal operations. Patrol beats. |
| Cypress Flats | Industrial. Low civilian presence. Criminal operations. |
| LSPD La Mesa Substation | Secondary police presence in the east. |

### Port of Los Santos / Elysian Island
**IC significance:** The backbone of import/export RP and smuggling.

| Location | IC Purpose |
|---|---|
| Terminal docks | Cargo arrivals, smuggling, high-value contraband. GM events, criminal operations. |
| Elysian Island warehouses | Criminal stash locations, gang operations, isolated meets. |
| Port Authority | Law enforcement, customs, coast guard equivalent. |

## Blaine County — Rural Areas

### Sandy Shores
**IC significance:** Off-grid criminal territory. Methamphetamine production, survivalist culture.

| Location | IC Purpose |
|---|---|
| Sandy Shores Airfield | Light aviation, criminal logistics air transport. Uncontrolled airspace. |
| Alamo Sea | Remote crime scenes, body disposal, isolated negotiations. |
| BCSO Sandy Shores Station | Blaine County Sheriff's presence. Rural patrol headquarters. |

### Grapeseed
**IC significance:** Agricultural community. Quiet, civilian, farm RP.

| Location | IC Purpose |
|---|---|
| Grapeseed farms | Farm RP, agricultural crime (crop theft, etc.), civilian character backstory. |
| McKenzie Airfield | Small grass strip. Smuggling drops, bush flying RP. |

### Paleto Bay
**IC significance:** Northernmost community. Isolated, close to forest and mountains.

| Location | IC Purpose |
|---|---|
| Paleto Bay Bank | Remote bank. Less LEO response time — high priority target. |
| Paleto Bay BCSO | Sheriff's station serving the north coast. |
| Procopio Beach | Isolated north coast. Smuggling drops, remote meets. |

### Fort Zancudo
**IC significance:** Military installation. Restricted access — serious RP consequences for unauthorized entry.

| Location | IC Purpose |
|---|---|
| Fort Zancudo base | All military RP. SAMAF operations, airfield, armory, command. |
| Restricted airspace | Unauthorized aircraft face IC military response. |

## Key Drive Routes

| Route | IC Use |
|---|---|
| **Great Ocean Highway** | Coastal scenic route. Patrol, pursuits, civilian traffic. |
| **The freeway (I-5 equivalent)** | High-speed pursuits, trafficking routes, commuter traffic. |
| **Senora Freeway** | Desert route to Sandy Shores. Criminal transport, rural patrol. |
| **Route 68** | Main road through Blaine County. |
| **Paleto highway** | North coast road. Remote, limited LEO coverage. |

## Greenzones (No Hostile RP)

Most servers designate these as protected zones:

| Location | Reason |
|---|---|
| LSMC / Pillbox Hill Medical Center | Hospital grounds — no violence at a place of healing |
| MRPD / Police stations | Station grounds — criminal activity directly in front of a police station is cop baiting |
| Spawn areas | Players just entering the world should not be immediately victimized |
| Some servers: the pier, the beach | Designated civilian safe areas |

Always check your server's specific greenzone rules.
