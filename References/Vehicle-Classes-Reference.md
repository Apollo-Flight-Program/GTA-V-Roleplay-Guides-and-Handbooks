# Vehicle Classes Reference

Vehicles in GTA V roleplay are more than transportation — they communicate who your character is, what they do for a living, and where they fall in the social hierarchy of the city. A beat-up Buccaneer in Davis tells a very different story than a Paragon R in Rockford Hills.

This reference covers every major vehicle class, their common models, real-life equivalents, and how they are typically used in roleplay.

---

| Albany Washington | Lincoln Town Car | Classic sedan. Often used as a taxi or livery vehicle. |

## Vehicle Choice by Character Type
