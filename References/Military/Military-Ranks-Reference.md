# Military Ranks Reference

A complete reference of military ranks used in GTA V roleplay, organized by branch. Servers may adapt these as needed, but this represents the standard structure used by most serious military RP communities.

---

## How Ranks Work

Military rank determines authority, responsibility, and privileges. It is not just a title — it represents experience, training, and leadership capacity.

**Two tracks:**
- **Enlisted** — The backbone of the military. Soldiers, specialists, and NCOs who carry out the work.
- **Officers** — Leadership and command. Commissioned through training academies or officer programs.

**Warrant Officers** occupy a specialized technical/leadership track between enlisted and commissioned officers. Used primarily in aviation and intelligence roles.

## Army / Ground Forces Ranks

### Enlisted

| Grade | Rank | Abbreviation | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| E-1 | Private | PVT | Entry level. No authority. |
| E-2 | Private Second Class | PV2 | Basic promotion after initial training. |
| E-3 | Private First Class | PFC | Shows sustained performance. |
| E-4 | Specialist | SPC | Technical specialists. Equivalent to Corporal in some structures. |
| E-4 | Corporal | CPL | First leadership role. Leads small fire teams. |
| E-5 | Sergeant | SGT | First true NCO rank. Leads fire teams. |
| E-6 | Staff Sergeant | SSG | Leads a squad. Senior NCO. |
| E-7 | Sergeant First Class | SFC | Platoon sergeant. Key advisor to platoon leader. |
| E-8 | Master Sergeant | MSG | Senior advisory NCO. |
| E-8 | First Sergeant | 1SG | Senior NCO in a company. "Top." Critical role. |
| E-9 | Sergeant Major | SGM | Battalion/Brigade level senior NCO. |
| E-9 | Command Sergeant Major | CSM | Senior advisor to the commanding officer at battalion/brigade. |
| E-9 | Sergeant Major of the Army | SMA | Most senior enlisted. One per army. |

### Warrant Officers

| Grade | Rank | Abbreviation | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| W-1 | Warrant Officer 1 | WO1 | Entry warrant. Usually aviation cadets. |
| W-2 | Chief Warrant Officer 2 | CW2 | Qualified specialist. |
| W-3 | Chief Warrant Officer 3 | CW3 | Senior specialist. |
| W-4 | Chief Warrant Officer 4 | CW4 | Master level specialist. |
| W-5 | Chief Warrant Officer 5 | CW5 | Most senior warrant. Technical master. |

### Commissioned Officers

| Grade | Rank | Abbreviation | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| O-1 | Second Lieutenant | 2LT | Entry officer. Leads a platoon. Often newest and least experienced. |
| O-2 | First Lieutenant | 1LT | Experienced platoon leader. Often XO of a company. |
| O-3 | Captain | CPT | Commands a company (80–200 soldiers). |
| O-4 | Major | MAJ | Staff officer. Often operations or executive officer at battalion. |
| O-5 | Lieutenant Colonel | LTC | Commands a battalion (300–1,000 soldiers). |
| O-6 | Colonel | COL | Commands a brigade (3,000–5,000 soldiers). |
| O-7 | Brigadier General | BG | One-star general. |
| O-8 | Major General | MG | Two-star general. Division command. |
| O-9 | Lieutenant General | LTG | Three-star. Corps command. |
| O-10 | General | GEN | Four-star. Army command. |

## Addressing Ranks

| Situation | How to Address |
|---|---|
| Enlisted (E1–E4) | "Private," "Specialist" |
| NCOs (E5–E9) | "Sergeant," "Staff Sergeant," "First Sergeant," "Sergeant Major" |
| Warrant Officers | "Mister/Ms. [Last Name]" or "Chief" (W3 and above) |
| Company-grade officers (O1–O3) | "Lieutenant" (for 2LT and 1LT), "Captain" |
| Field-grade officers (O4–O6) | "Major," "Colonel" |
| General officers | "General" |

**Key customs:**
- All enlisted address officers as "Sir" or "Ma'am" regardless of specific rank
- NCOs are addressed by their rank title, not "Sir" or "Ma'am"
- Warrant Officers are typically addressed as "Chief"
- Never call a Sergeant "Sir" — they have earned their rank by working, not by appointment

## SAMAF Rank Adaptation

Most GTA V roleplay servers adapt real-world ranks to their SAMAF structure. Common simplifications:

- Start enlisted track at PVT or PFC rather than having all nine enlisted grades
- Compress warrant officer track — often just CW1, CW2, CW3
- Cap officer track at Colonel or Brigadier General for practical server hierarchy
- Adapt Air Force ranks to the in-universe "San Andreas Military Air Force" structure

Check your server's specific rank structure in your faction handbook.
