# Los Santos Map Guide

The world of GTA V roleplay takes place across two major regions: Los Santos, a sprawling metropolitan city based on Los Angeles, and Blaine County, the rural and desert region to the north. Knowing the map is essential. Where you are shapes who you meet, what kind of RP you encounter, and how your character is perceived.

This guide covers the key neighborhoods, landmarks, and RP hotspots that every player should know.

---

## Los Santos — City Overview

Los Santos is divided into distinct neighborhoods, each with its own character, reputation, and the type of roleplay it naturally generates. Think of it as a living city — the wealthy hills, the working-class flats, the dangerous south side, and everything in between.

---

## Vinewood & Vinewood Hills

**Real-life equivalent:** Hollywood and the Hollywood Hills

The entertainment capital of Los Santos. Vinewood is where celebrities, wealthy characters, and tourists congregate. The iconic Vinewood Sign overlooks the area from the hills above.

| Location | Description |
|---|---|
| Vinewood Boulevard | The main strip. Theaters, shops, bars. High foot traffic and tourist RP. |
| Vinewood Sign | Iconic landmark. Scenic RP and meetup point. |
| Oriental Theater | Premiere venue. Event RP location for movie premieres and shows. |
| Tequi-La-La | Bar/nightclub on the boulevard. A common social RP hub. |
| Vinewood Hills Mansions | Luxury residences. Home to wealthy characters, celebrities, and crime bosses. |
| Galileo Observatory | Hilltop observatory. Romantic RP, scenic meetups, and occasionally criminal dealings after dark. |

**Common RP:** Celebrity encounters, nightlife, paparazzi, parties, luxury living, tourism.

---

## Rockford Hills

**Real-life equivalent:** Beverly Hills

The wealthiest residential area in Los Santos. Gated properties, manicured lawns, and luxury vehicles on every street. This is where the city's elite live.

| Location | Description |
|---|---|
| Rockford Hills residences | Multi-million dollar homes. The address signals extreme wealth. |
| Vangelico Jewelry Store | High-end jewelry store. A common robbery target in criminal RP. |
| Ponsonbys | Designer clothing store. Shopping RP for upscale characters. |
| Michael's House (Portola Drive) | Iconic story mode location sometimes used as a reference point. |

**Common RP:** Wealthy civilian life, business dealings, high-society events, robberies targeting the affluent.

---

## Del Perro & Vespucci

**Real-life equivalent:** Santa Monica and Venice Beach

The beachfront district. Del Perro Beach and the Vespucci Boardwalk are among the most populated areas on any server. Casual, social, and occasionally chaotic.

| Location | Description |
|---|---|
| Del Perro Pier | Ferris wheel, arcade, and boardwalk. One of the top social RP locations. |
| Vespucci Beach | The boardwalk area. Street performers, vendors, and beach life RP. |
| Del Perro Heights | Apartment complex. A common player housing location. |
| Pacific Bluffs | Coastal residential area west of Del Perro. Quieter, upscale. |
| Maze Bank Arena | Event venue. Used for organized fights, concerts, and server events. |

**Common RP:** Beach life, surfing culture, street performance, casual socializing, boardwalk confrontations.

---

## Little Seoul

**Real-life equivalent:** Koreatown, Los Angeles

A dense urban neighborhood with a strong cultural identity. Neon signs, tight streets, and a mix of legitimate businesses and criminal activity.

| Location | Description |
|---|---|
| Little Seoul streets | Dense urban area with restaurants, shops, and apartments. |
| Korean Plaza | Strip mall area. Hub for small business RP. |
| Maze Bank Tower | The tallest building in Los Santos. Corporate RP, executive offices, and the most prestigious address in the city. |

**Common RP:** Cultural RP, small business ownership, gang activity, nightlife.

---

## Downtown Los Santos

**Real-life equivalent:** Downtown Los Angeles

The commercial and governmental heart of the city. Skyscrapers, government buildings, and the financial district create a backdrop for legal, political, and corporate roleplay.

| Location | Description |
|---|---|
| City Hall | The center of government RP. Mayoral events, press conferences, protests, and political campaigns. |
| Los Santos Courthouse | Where court cases are heard. Defense attorneys, prosecutors, judges, and juries. Essential for DOJ RP. |
| Weazel News Building | Media headquarters. Reporter and journalist RP hub. |
| Maze Bank Tower | Financial headquarters. Business meetings and corporate deals. |
| Arcadius Business Center | Secondary office tower. Used by various businesses and law firms. |

**Common RP:** Government proceedings, court trials, business meetings, journalism, protests and rallies.

---

## Mission Row

**Real-life equivalent:** No direct equivalent — a gritty downtown precinct area

The most important law enforcement location in the city. Mission Row is home to the LSPD's main station and is the heart of police RP.

| Location | Description |
|---|---|
| Mission Row Police Department (MRPD) | The primary police station. Booking, interrogation, briefing rooms, evidence lockup, and the front desk. The center of LEO RP. |
| Holding Cells | Where arrested suspects are processed and held before transfer or release. |
| Parking Garage | Where patrol vehicles are staged. Officers begin and end their shifts here. |

**Common RP:** Arrests, bookings, interrogations, shift briefings, detective work, suspect processing.

---

## Pillbox Hill

**Real-life equivalent:** General hospital district

Home to the city's main hospital and the hub of all EMS roleplay.

| Location | Description |
|---|---|
| Pillbox Hill Medical Center | The primary hospital. Emergency room, operating rooms, patient wards. All medical RP flows through here. |
| Mount Zonah Medical Center | Secondary hospital in the area. Some servers use this as the main medical facility. |
| Pharmacy | Prescription pickups and medical supply RP. |

**Common RP:** Medical emergencies, doctor-patient interactions, ambulance arrivals, recovery RP, psychological evaluations.

---

## Strawberry

**Real-life equivalent:** South Central (Inglewood area)

A working-class to low-income neighborhood. Strawberry is one of the primary gang RP territories and is known for its gritty, street-level roleplay.

| Location | Description |
|---|---|
| Strawberry Avenue | Main road through the neighborhood. Frequent police activity and foot traffic. |
| Davis Avenue (border) | The line between Strawberry and Davis. Gang boundary disputes happen here. |
| Residential blocks | Row houses and apartments. Home to many civilian and criminal characters. |

**Common RP:** Gang RP, street crime, community life, police patrols, neighborhood disputes.

---

## Davis

**Real-life equivalent:** Compton

The most notorious neighborhood in Los Santos for criminal RP. Davis is the home of several major gangs and is one of the most dangerous areas on the map.

| Location | Description |
|---|---|
| Grove Street | Iconic gang territory. One of the most recognizable streets in GTA. |
| Davis Police Station | The local precinct. LSPD or BCSO presence depending on the server. |
| Ballas Territory | The eastern blocks of Davis. Purple gang territory. |
| Families Territory | Grove Street and surroundings. Green gang territory. |
| Davis Mega Mall | Shopping area. Neutral ground for commerce, but tensions remain. |

**Common RP:** Gang wars, drug operations, territorial disputes, community outreach, police raids.

---

## Mirror Park

**Real-life equivalent:** Silver Lake / Echo Park, Los Angeles

A quiet, hipster-friendly neighborhood northeast of downtown. Mirror Park has a suburban feel with a creative community.

| Location | Description |
|---|---|
| Mirror Park Lake | Scenic lakeside area. Fishing RP, casual conversation, relaxation. |
| Mirror Park residences | Bungalows and small homes. Popular with creative and middle-class characters. |
| Mirror Park Boulevard | Local shops and cafes. A calmer social environment. |

**Common RP:** Suburban life, artistic/creative characters, peaceful civilian RP, neighborhood events.

---

## La Mesa & East Los Santos

**Real-life equivalent:** East Los Angeles

An industrial and working-class area east of downtown. Known for warehouses, auto shops, and a significant criminal presence.

| Location | Description |
|---|---|
| Harmony Repairs / Auto shops | Mechanic RP hubs. Vehicle customization and repair. |
| Industrial warehouses | Used for drug labs, storage, and criminal operations. |
| La Mesa Police Station | Local precinct for the eastern part of the city. |
| Power Station | Los Santos Department of Water and Power. Industrial RP. |

**Common RP:** Mechanic work, warehouse dealings, drug manufacturing, industrial crime.

---

## Blaine County — Sandy Shores

**Real-life equivalent:** Desert communities (Salton Sea area)

The largest settlement in the desert region of Blaine County. Sandy Shores is home to bikers, survivalists, rural civilians, and anyone who prefers life outside the city.

| Location | Description |
|---|---|
| Sandy Shores Sheriff's Office | The main law enforcement outpost in the desert. BCSO jurisdiction. |
| Sandy Shores Medical Center | Rural clinic. Limited staff, basic medical RP. |
| Trevor's Trailer | Iconic story mode location. Often used as a reference point. |
| Alamo Sea | The large lake in the region. Fishing, boating, and body-dumping RP. |
| Altruist Camp | Cult-themed hilltop camp. Used for horror and fringe RP scenarios. |
| Sandy Shores Airfield | Small airstrip. Smuggling operations, private flights, and pilot RP. |

**Common RP:** MC club operations, meth manufacturing, rural crime, survivalism, fishing, off-road recreation.

---

## Blaine County — Grapeseed

**Real-life equivalent:** Small Central California farming towns

A small agricultural community north of Sandy Shores. Grapeseed is the quietest area on most servers and is ideal for farming and rural life RP.

| Location | Description |
|---|---|
| Grapeseed Main Street | The small town center. General store, gas station, and a few buildings. |
| Farmland | Open fields surrounding the town. Farming and ranch RP. |
| McKenzie Airfield | Small dirt airstrip. Crop dusters and private aviation. |

**Common RP:** Farming, rural civilian life, small-town community, bootlegging, isolation RP.

---

## Blaine County — Paleto Bay

**Real-life equivalent:** Small Pacific Coast towns

The northernmost town in the map. Paleto Bay is a small coastal community with a sheriff's substation, a few businesses, and a quiet pace of life.

| Location | Description |
|---|---|
| Paleto Bay Sheriff's Office | BCSO substation. The northernmost law enforcement outpost. |
| Paleto Bay Medical | Small-town clinic. |
| Paleto Bay Bank | One of the banks in the game. Robbery target for criminal RP. |
| Cluckin' Bell Factory | Chicken processing plant. Industrial and blue-collar RP. |
| Paleto Forest | Dense forest area. Hunting RP, camping, and hiding from the law. |
| Procopio Beach | Northern coastline. Quiet beach RP. |

**Common RP:** Small-town life, coastal living, logging, fishing, isolation, hiding from city problems.
