# Penal Code Template

This is a template penal code for GTA V roleplay servers. It is designed to be adapted, expanded, or trimmed to fit your server's specific culture and rules. All charge names, fine amounts, and sentence lengths are suggestions — adjust them to match your server's economy and punishment philosophy.

---

## How to Use This Template

1. Copy this document into your server's handbook or wiki
2. Adjust fine amounts to match your server's in-game economy
3. Adjust sentence lengths to match your server's jail/prison system
4. Add or remove charges to fit your server's lore and active crime types
5. Share it with your law enforcement department and legal team (if applicable)

---

## Charge Classification

| Class | Description | Typical Sentence |
|---|---|---|
| **Infraction** | Minor violation, no criminal record | Fine only |
| **Misdemeanor** | Low-level offense | Fine + short custody |
| **Felony** | Serious offense | Significant jail time |
| **Capital Offense** | Most severe crimes | Maximum sentence |

## Sentencing Notes

**Concurrent vs. Consecutive Sentences**
When a suspect is charged with multiple offenses, sentences may be served concurrently (at the same time) or consecutively (one after another). Most servers cap total sentence length — define your cap here.

> Recommended cap: **120 months maximum** regardless of charge count.

**Charge Stacking**
Officers may charge a suspect with multiple applicable offenses from a single incident. For example, an armed robbery resulting in injury could carry charges 2.05, 1.02, and 4.04 simultaneously.

**Reduced Charges**
Officers and legal staff may negotiate reduced charges in exchange for cooperation, information, or a guilty plea — at their discretion and within server rules.

**Dismissed Charges**
A judge or senior legal official may dismiss charges if evidence is insufficient or if proper arrest procedure was not followed.

## Notes for Server Owners

- Sentence lengths are in **in-game months** — adjust to your server's time conversion (e.g. 1 month = 1 real minute in custody)
- Fine amounts should reflect your server's economy — if players routinely carry $50,000, a $150 speeding fine is meaningless
- Consider adding server-specific charges relevant to your lore (military zone trespassing, gang territory laws, etc.)
- This template does not cover civil law — add a separate civil code if your server has a functioning legal system
