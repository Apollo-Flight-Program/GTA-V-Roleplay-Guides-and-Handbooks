# 10-Codes Reference Sheet

10-codes (also called ten-codes) are numeric radio codes used by law enforcement and emergency services to communicate quickly and clearly over radio. They were standardized in 1940 by the Association of Public-Safety Communications Officials (APCO) and remain widely used in roleplay communities to maintain realism and immersion.

Different regions and agencies use slightly different variations. This sheet covers the most commonly used codes in GTA V roleplay.

|---|---|
| Signal 0 | Officer needs immediate assistance |

| Signal 2 | Proceed normally |
| Signal 3 | Use no lights or siren |
| Signal 4 | No further assistance needed |

---

## Usage Notes

- Always state your unit number before transmitting: *"Dispatch, Unit 14, 10-20 is downtown near the courthouse."*
- Keep transmissions short and clear — do not use 10-codes in casual conversation, only on radio
- Not all servers use the same codes — always check your server's specific code sheet
- When unsure, plain language is always acceptable over radio
